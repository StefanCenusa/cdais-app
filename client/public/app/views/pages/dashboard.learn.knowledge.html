Debatabase

Debatabase is a starting point on the road to participating in debates. The volume provides a beginning for those debaters who would like to learn about important topics being argued in the public sphere. Debaters can use this volume as a method of discovering the basic issues relevant to some of the more important topics being discussed in various public forums. It will provide debaters a brief look at some of the claims that can be used to support or to oppose many of the issues argued about by persons in democratic societies; it will also provide some sketches of evidence that can be used to support these claims. Platon - Republica

Republica este o lucrare (dialog socratic) scrisă de Platon aproximativ în anul 360 î.Hr. Este o lucrare influentă de filozofie și de teorie politică, probabil și cea mai cunoscută lucrare a lui. Republica
